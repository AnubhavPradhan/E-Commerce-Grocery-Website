References

Heading box

The website we took inspiration from is called 'abelandcole' and has a similar e-commerce theme. The header box was highly eye-catching, simple, and attractive, so we coded, effectively visualized and implanted it in our style.

Footer

Particularly, the footer area of Islington's website and Homely Harvest have a similar aesthetic look. The company logo of Homely Harvest is shown on the left beside that like Islington's website, and information about the website is presented in a manner similar to that of Islington's website, serving as the reference for the footer design.

Why us section

The section 'Why us?' is made by taking reference from a website called 'GloballyCool'.You can see similar type of concept being used in our website. The concept is even a bit enhanced in our website, it looks a bit better, clean and aesthetic.

Products Layout

The website we took inspiration for our products page is called 'merokirana' which sells various grocery items. The box was really simple and neat and we coded and visualized the image and button placement in our own way. The product box is very simple and there were not too much stuffs cramped in it. In comparison, 'merokirana' focuses on selling different types of products, our product page only focuses on selling organic fruits and vegetables.
